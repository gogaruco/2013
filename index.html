Save the dates!

GoGaRuCo 2012 is coming…

September 14-15, 2012

UCSF Mission Bay

Join Us for the
3rd Annual GoGaRuCo!

San Francisco is the birthplace of the web revolution. Ruby powers many new technologies, from web sites to telephony and desktop applications. Join us for two days of in-depth technical explorations of the most interesting parts of the Ruby landscape, presented by some of the top talents in the Ruby development community.

Registration for the second annual Golden Gate Ruby Conference is open now! Your ticket to Golden Gate Ruby Conference gets you the following:

  • Admission to two days of sessions
  • Breakfast and Lunch both days
  • Official Conference swag
  • Access to after-hours parties
  • Access to our fantastic attendees and speakers

The conference is single-track. Just like last year we're doing 30-minute sessions, to ensure that talks stay focused and interesting, with long breaks to allow time for networking and discussion.

See you all there!

Event Updates

Parties Announced, Registration Opened!

Posted July 11, 2011

The time has arrived! Registration for GoGaRuCo is now open! If you poke around the site you'll find info on our keynotes, first-announced speakers (more to follow!), details on the venue, and more. We wanted to take this space to call out something new though: Parties!

Join Platinum Sponsor Square Inc. and fellow Rubyists for free drinks, appetizers and entertainment. Friday, September 16th, 7:00pm. Venue and registration details to follow.

Platinum Sponsor Yammer will be hosting a Saturday, September 17th conference wrap party, with a similar drinks + snacks setup. Details to be announced.

Call For Proposals is Open

Posted June 1, 2011

It's that time: we're back! GoGaRuCo 2011 will happen September 16-17. Following our tradition of trying something new each year, this year we're soliciting proposals for talks. If you have something awesome to share with us, submit a proposal online by June 18th.
